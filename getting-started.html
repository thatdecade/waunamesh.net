Getting started

Join the Waunakee mesh.

Start with a small LoRa radio, install MeshCore, introduce yourself to the community, and send your first message.

1

Introduce yourself.

Introduce yourself and mention that you are in Waunakee or interested in helping the regional network grow.

Join the Madison Mesh Discord
2

Pick a MeshCore compatible device.

The Heltec V3 requires assembly and is a good starter option for experimenting, learning the app, and sending your first messages in Wisconsin. While the ThinkNode M1 or Wio Traker L1 Pro are good turnkey options. Visit the MeshCore hardware list for additional options.

3

Install the MeshCore app.

Get the MeshCore app
4

Flash MeshCore firmware.

  1. Open the MeshCore Web Flasher in desktop Chrome or Edge, connect your radio by USB, and select your device.
  2. Choose the MeshCore companion firmware for your device, click Flash, then pair the radio with the MeshCore app.
MeshCore Web Flasher
5

Say hello and test coverage.

Once the app and radio are paired, try a short public hello message, walk around, and watch what nodes your radio can hear. If you are unsure what to do next, ask in the Discord and share what device you bought.
