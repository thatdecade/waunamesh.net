Other mesh communities

WaunaMesh is local, but we are not alone.

Other communities share ideas about mapping, host sites, field days, public outreach, and practical radio builds. WaunaMesh uses MeshCore, but these groups are still useful inspiration.

Parent community

Madison Mesh

The regional community WaunaMesh links into. Start here for Madison-area Discord, mapping, and nearby help.

Official resource

MeshCore

The official MeshCore website for downloads, supported hardware, the flasher, docs, and current project links.
